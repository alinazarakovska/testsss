Robotics

// Robotics is a whole science, in robotics you can be both a "screenwriter" and a "director" you have to design, think about details, robotics requires a lot of brain activity. Not only physics is important in robotics, but also creativity and actually all the knowledges are very important.

Robotics provides:

  • Development of fine motor skills when working with small parts;
  • Develops math and counting skills: even at the robot selection level, you have to deal with beams of different lengths and compare parts by size;
  • First programming experience: design skills;
  • Teamwork: The robot is usually done together;
  • Presentation skills: Once the project is complete, talk about it.

Volleyball

// At our school, of course, there is also an opportunity for students to express themselves in sports and directly - in Volleyball.

Why exactly Volleyball?

Volleyball teaches communication, effective communication. During the game, the team members understand each other perfectly, this kind of coherence often becomes the decisive factor and a way to beat even the strongest opponents. Players on the field will soon become friends, helpers, business partners, advisors outside training. This means that everyone's social circle is expanding. And in our time, the more friends, the more opportunities!

Volleyball tactics are important. Thinking about the movements of competitors, guessing, thinking and acting - such skills will be useful not only in sports, but also in life.
